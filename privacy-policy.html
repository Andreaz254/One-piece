Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

Your privacy is of utmost importance to One Piece Books. We are committed to protecting the personal information you provide when using our website and purchasing our digital products. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information Collection

  • We only collect information essential to process purchases, such as your email address and purchase details.
  • We do not store or process credit card information directly; all payments are handled securely by trusted payment processors.

2. Use of Information

  • To deliver purchased digital products and communicate order confirmations or updates.
  • To improve our services and website experience.

3. Information Sharing

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • Data may be shared with service providers solely for processing payments and delivering digital products securely.

4. Data Security

  • We implement strict administrative, technical, and physical measures to safeguard your personal information.
  • All sensitive data is transmitted over secure connections (HTTPS) and handled per industry best practices.

5. Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
  • You have the right to opt out of marketing communications at any time.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact: support@skunaone.tech
